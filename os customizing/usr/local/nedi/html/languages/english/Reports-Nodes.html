Node Reports

Reports focused around the anything connected to your network.

SummaryNode statistics at a glance
Node DistributionDistribution of nodes by port and device to detect unmanaged switches or hubs
Duplicate NodesShows duplicate node names (e.g. having a Wlan and Ethernet connection) or MAC addresses
Node AddressShows duplicate or multiple IP addresses
OS & ServicesShow top node OS and type statistics if nodes are identified with NeDi's scan feature
NomadsIP and IF changes multiplied yield NeDi's nomad factor, an indicator for those who seem to travel a lot
Discover HistoryThis hisory can reveal major changes or problems in your network. Each coloumn is limited individually (use filter to narrow down the timeframe)
Empty VlansUnpopulated vlans can be identified and eventually removed, if not needed on particular devices
